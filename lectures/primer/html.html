Hypertext Markup Language

HTML — the unifying living standard markup language of web is roughly only about 30 years old. However, over the past 25 years, HTML has gone through some major changes, and hasn’t always been unified. There’s been about five major versions: HTML 2.0 (1994), HTML 3.2 (1997), HTML 4.01 (1999), XHTML 1.0 (2000) and HTML5 (2009, onward).
